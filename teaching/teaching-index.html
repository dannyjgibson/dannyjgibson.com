Hi there, I'm Mr. Gibson!

Here's the syllabus for my current Geometry class.

Students can make up Exit Tickets as they want/need.

Here are the instructions:

  1. Exit tickets are the majority of your grade.
  2. Find a exit ticket with a low score from your tracker.
  3. Find the make-up tickets below or in the class-room binder.
  4. Retake the exit ticket.
    1. Work on your own paper or copy.
    2. Write the questions.
    3. Turn in to the exit ticket bin.
    4. If you don't feel ready to retake an exit ticket:
      • Lunch or after school with Mr. Gibson
      • Math tutoring in Room 100, Monday -- Thursday, 4pm -- 5pm
      • Saturday School, 8 AM -- 11 AM
      • Transportation can be coordinated to any of the above.
